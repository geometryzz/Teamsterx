Promote to Admin

Promote to Admin?

Admins can:

  • Invite new members
  • Approve/deny join requests
  • Manage tasks and events

They will lose:

  • Ability to invite new members
  • Ability to approve/deny join requests
  • Admin management privileges
  • Will remain as a regular team member
